# Unit Converter

Website to convert units of different types; like meters to kilometers, or years to seconds.

## Unit Types

The unit types available in this unit converter are:

- Length
- Area
- Volume
- Mass
- Time
- Energy
- Temperature

## Conversions

### Length

The following units of length are supported:

- Meter
- Kilometer
- Centimeter
- Millimeter
- Micrometer
- Nanometer
- Mile
- Yard
- Foot
- Inch
- Nautical Mile

### Area

The following units of area are supported:

- Square meter
- Square kilometer
- Square mile
- Square yard
- Square foot
- Square inch
- Hectare
- Acre

### Volume

- US liquid gallon

- US liquid cup

- Imperial quart
- Imperial pint
- Imperial cup
- Imperial fluid ounce
- Imperial tablespoon
- Imperial teaspoon
- US tablespoon
- US teaspoon
- Cubic foot
- Cubic inch
- Cubic meter
- Liter

### Mass
